Fitness for Every Woman

Our Programs

At SAU FITNESS, we offer specialized fitness programs designed to support women through every stage of life. Whether your goal is weight management, strength building, wellness, or maintaining an active lifestyle, we have a program for you.

Teen Fitness Program (13–19 Years)

Build healthy habits, confidence, and physical strength in a safe and supportive environment.

Focus Areas:

  • Basic strength training
  • Cardiovascular fitness
  • Flexibility and mobility
  • Healthy lifestyle education
  • Confidence building

Student & Young Women's Fitness

Designed for busy students and young professionals looking to stay active and energized.

Focus Areas:

  • Full-body workouts
  • Weight management
  • Strength training
  • Stress relief and mental wellness
  • Endurance training

Women's Strength & Conditioning

For women who want to build strength, improve fitness, and enhance overall health.

Focus Areas:

  • Resistance training
  • Functional fitness
  • Core strengthening
  • Weight loss and toning
  • Cardiovascular conditioning

Prenatal Fitness Program

Safe and supportive workouts for expectant mothers. *Participation is recommended with approval from your healthcare provider.

Focus Areas:

  • Pregnancy-safe exercises
  • Mobility and flexibility
  • Core and pelvic floor support
  • Improved posture
  • Preparation for childbirth

Postnatal Recovery Program

Helping mothers regain strength and confidence safely after childbirth.

Focus Areas:

  • Core restoration
  • Pelvic floor strengthening
  • Gentle conditioning
  • Posture improvement
  • Gradual strength rebuilding

Yoga & Wellness Classes

Improve flexibility, reduce stress, and enhance overall well-being.

Focus Areas:

  • Stretching
  • Balance
  • Mindfulness
  • Relaxation techniques
  • Breathing exercises

Weight Management Program

A balanced approach to achieving and maintaining a healthy weight long-term.

Focus Areas:

  • Personalized workout plans
  • Fat-loss training
  • Nutritional guidance
  • Progress tracking
  • Lifestyle coaching

Active Aging Program (50+ Years)

Designed to help mature women stay strong, mobile, and fully independent.

Focus Areas:

  • Low-impact exercises
  • Balance and coordination
  • Joint mobility
  • Strength maintenance
  • Flexibility training

Group Fitness Classes

Stay motivated and have fun while working out with a vibrant sisterhood.

Classes May Include:

  • Aerobics & Dance Fitness
  • HIIT Workouts & Circuit Training
  • Strength Classes
  • Stretch & Recovery Sessions

Personal Training (1-on-1)

Receive highly personalized, one-on-one guidance completely tailored to your unique biology, history, and fitness benchmarks.

Perfect For:

  • Beginners
  • Weight loss goals
  • Strength building
  • Postpartum fitness
