The Heart Behind the Movement

Meet Coach Sau Janice

I don’t believe in boring, rigid rules. I believe in helping you fall in love with your own power.

As a trainer, I watched too many people get overwhelmed by aggressive, intimidating gym cultures and generic plans that don't fit real life. I created SAU FITNESS to turn the page. This is a high-energy, vibrant community space built around functional movement, dynamic strength training, and absolute confidence.

Whether you are restarting your journey, building up core athletic durability, or learning to lift for the very first time, I tailor every architecture to match your unique lifestyle pacing. Let's make lifestyle transformation fun, intense, and completely rewarding.
